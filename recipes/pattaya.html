Nasi Goreng Pattaya

Ingredients For Fried Rice

  • Rice, white
  • Oil, vegetable
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Chilli Paste
  • Egg
  • Chicken Breast, cubed
  • Prawns, Shelled
  • Fresh Squid, medium
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • White Pepper Powder
  • Knorr Pasti Sedap 600G

Ingredients for Egg Wrap for Nasi Pattaya:

  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Knorr Pasti Sedap 600G
  • Oil, vegetable
  • Chilli Sauce
  • Scallion
  • Fried Shallots

Preperation

  1. Stir fry the chopped garlic together with chilli paste in preheated oil.
  2. Add in chicken breast, prawns, and squid and stir until cooked.
  3. Mix in egg.
  4. Pour in rice along with light soy sauce, fish sauce, white pepper, and Knorr Pasti Sedap.
  5. Mix the eggs with Knorr Pasti Sedap.
  6. Pour the egg mixture in a hot wok, and turn off the stove.
  7. Spread out the egg mixture around the inner wall of the wok.
  8. Put in your fried rice in the middle of the thinned out omelette and fold the egg.
  9. Sprinkle the garnish on the Nasi Goreng Pattaya.
  10. Flavourful Nasi Goreng Pattaya is ready to serve.
